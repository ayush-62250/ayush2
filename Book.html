My Favorite Books & Why I Recommend Them

  • Productivity Superhero by Dan Luca
    To become the most organized and disciplined person you know.
  • 48 Laws of Power by Robert Greene
    A guide to mastering power through 48 laws, blending historical and psychological insights with examples from notable figures.
  • Clean Code by Robert C. Martin
    Essential for any software engineer who wants to write better, maintainable code.
  • The Lean Startup by Eric Ries
    Introduces the MVP approach and helps founders and engineers think iteratively.
  • Zero to One by Peter Thiel
    Insightful lessons on innovation, startups, and creating something truly new.
